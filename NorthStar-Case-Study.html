NorthStar Vision Summit



Problem

NorthStar vision summit for a $101 billion enterprise business, consisting of a series of 5 workshops with Sr. Directors, SVPâ€™s, other various stakeholders, and Executive Sponsors, and then 5 more workshops that cascaded down from the initial 5 workshops for Product Managers, Directors, Product Owners, and Executive Sponsors. The initial consultancy that was hired facilitate the summit backed out and the workshops needed to be conducted in a time span of four weeks for budgeting purposes for the upcoming year.

Solution

For the first time the business turned to the design organization and asked if internal product designer could facilitate and conduct the NorthStar visions summit. I was tasked with leading a team of three in conducting the workshops.

Impact

Setup a unifying strategy for the entire $101 billion enterprise business. Aligning the 5 business verticals that overlap and produced the strategy for the upcoming 3 and 5 years. Each of the 5 business verticals was worth an estimated $20 billion in the enterprise business.

Contraints

Design System not completely designed or coded, so we had to leverage the design system from AG Grid to speed up production and time to market.

The product team was in the initial stages of digital transformation, and as a design team, we had to help relationships to move them along that journey while proving .

Lessons learned


  • Prew-work for alignment is essential.
  • Assumptions exercise is critical to continue the alignment proces.
  • More time needed for discussion in both the guiding principles see and agree, and the assumptions discussions.
  • A time line of two weeks per NorthStar workshop prep work is needed for the design team.
  • Look into possible asynchronous ways of accomplishing some of the tasks in the workshop to allow for executive sponsors the ability to get their voices heard ahead of time.
  • Find a better way to emphasize the rule that unless all the main stakeholders that can derail the work done are attending then we punt to another day.
