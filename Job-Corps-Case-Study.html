Job Corps

Job Corps



Problem

Students are not aware of the opportunities that Job Corps offers for career trajetories, the benefits of attending job corps, and the applying and enrollment process.

Solution

The solution was a redesign the Job Corps national and local websites to create an intuitive enrollment experience, inform students on possible careers, and highlight the benefits of Job Corps achieve higher enrollments.

Impact

According to enrollment numbers from the Department of Labor, from 2017 to 2018, there was an increase in Job Corps enrollments by 8.14%, after the website redesign launch.

Storyboards



Created a storyboard to map out the users interaction with the website and what they hoped to accomplish. It helped with creating the task flow and the persona.


Persona



When the persona was put together, it consisted of overarching themes that applied to all potential job corps applicants, and based on research and sythesis of that research. It became apparent the critical path moving forward was to redesign website and streamline the access to the information.


User Task Flow



A task flow was created to figure out the critcal path that would solve the information and benefits flow based on data from the user research and the main persona.


Low Resolution Wireframes



Low resolution wireframes where created by the design team, in collaboration with business analyst, subject matter experts, developers, and project managers, to test, get feedback on, and solve for the wanted outcomes.


High Resolution Wireframes




High resolution wireframes where created by the design team, in collaboration with business analyst, subject matter experts, developers, and project managers, to test, get feedback on, send to production, and solve for the wanted outcomes.


Insights


The first insight was that for students it was critical to discover the benefits and assitance provided while attending Job Corps, so that could live and take care of their familites.

The second insight was that students really wanted to quickly and easily understand the value of a quick turn around time for learning a trade that could lead to a career.

The third insight was that if you streamline the process to find information and facilitate the enrollment process, students will enroll to make their lives better through a trade that lead to careers.
